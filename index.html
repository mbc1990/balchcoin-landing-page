Introducing BalchCoin

"I came here in 1624 as a humble yoeman, that my kids may live to be lawyers and doctors, that their kids may live to trade stocks, that their kids may live to be artists and musicians, that their kids may live to pioneer the world of blockchain technology" - John Balch, 1633

BalchCoin is the final realization of John Balch's dreams, created by his direct descendants.

It is a cryptocurrency supported by nearly 400 years of New England tradition and stability, a coin for those who recognize that while some things come and go, some simply stay right where they are.
